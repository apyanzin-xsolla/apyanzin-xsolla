## About Me

I am **Andrey Pyanzin**, Product Owner for **Shop Builder Platform**.

My focus is platform products, developer ecosystems, experimentation, and analytics. Right now my work is connected with Shop Builder: a platform direction for building and customizing game commerce experiences.

The long-term idea is close to **Shopify Apps for the gaming industry**: a flexible foundation where internal teams, game developers, and third-party creators can build, extend, and reuse commerce solutions faster.

## What I Build
